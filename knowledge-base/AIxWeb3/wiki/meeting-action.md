## Definition

Meeting Action converts governance/community meeting discussions into executable items with specific owners, deadlines, dependencies, and decision status. Meeting summaries without action owners quickly become useless records.

## Key Points

- "We should study this" is NOT an action; "Alice to organize three payment options by next Friday" IS an action
- AI must convert vague discussions into checkable items, not just summarize what was said
- Decision status tracking: decided, pending confirmation, needs voting, needs budget, needs legal/security review
- Items should link to original discussion context for accountability
