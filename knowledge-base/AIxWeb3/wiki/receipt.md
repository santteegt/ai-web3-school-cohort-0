## Definition

A Receipt is a credential after payment and delivery. It is not just "paid" — it should record who paid whom, amount, currency, task ID, quote ID, time, transaction hash, service result reference, and acceptance status.

## Key Points

- Without receipts, reconciliation, reputation, and disputes are all difficult
- Receipts become input for reputation: on-time payment, on-time delivery, and refunds are all extractable from receipts
- Dual-readable: humans understand the task and amount; machines parse task IDs, payment transactions, delivery hashes, acceptance status
- Also serves as evidence in dispute resolution
