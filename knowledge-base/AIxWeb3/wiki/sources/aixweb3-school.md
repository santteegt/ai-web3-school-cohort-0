## Summary

This source provides the handbook outline and conceptual framing for the AI × Web3 School program. It establishes the four-layer map: AI Foundations, Web3 Foundations, AI × Web3 Bridge, and Frontier Exploration. The framing argues that AI × Web3 is not two buzzwords placed together — a real system involves model context, agent execution, wallet permissions, on-chain transactions, payments, identity, and governance simultaneously. Looking from only one side (AI or Web3) produces blind spots.

## Notable Points

- "If you only look from the AI side, you will underestimate asset and permission risks. If you only look from the Web3 side, you will miss the complexity of models, context, and tool orchestration."
