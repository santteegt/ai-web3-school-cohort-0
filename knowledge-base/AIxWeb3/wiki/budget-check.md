## Definition

Budget Check reviews whether governance fund requests are clear, reasonable, and traceable. AI can help check completeness, milestone specificity, past funding delivery, amount-to-scope alignment, and duplicate requests — helping the community see issues clearly, not making automatic cuts.

## Key Points

- Key questions: what deliverables correspond to this money, is payment pace tied to milestones, are there verifiable deliveries, what happens on failure or delay?
- On-chain checks: does the receiving address belong to the proposer, is it a multi-sig, does it need vesting, are there historical anomalies (unusual withdrawals, incomplete grants)?
- Budget Check is not automatic rejection — it surfaces information for human community judgment
- AI marks gaps; humans decide whether to fund
