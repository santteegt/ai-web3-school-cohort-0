## Definition

Frontier Exploration combines AI Foundations and AI × Web3 Bridge knowledge into prototypes that can be built, demoed, and extended. The Week 2–4 hackathon track selection is drawn from these frontier categories.

## Frontier Tracks

- **Agentic Commerce** — agents discover services, negotiate tasks, complete payments, leave receipts; connects [[machine-payment]], [[agent-wallet]], [[agent-identity]]
- **Wallet / Permission** — product prototypes around programmable wallets, Session Keys, Policies, and Guards; connects [[agent-wallet]], [[guardrails]]
- **AI Security** — attack surfaces, permission isolation, audits, and alerts as demonstrable systems; connects [[ai-security]], [[prompt-injection]], [[guardrails]]
- **Governance** — AI collaboration tools for DAOs, public goods, and protocol governance; connects [[governance-ai]]
- **Dev Tooling** — contract understanding, testing, documentation, code review, and developer workflows; connects [[vibe-coding]], [[retrieval-augmented-generation]]
- **Open Track** — reserved for new intersection problems not yet in fixed categories
