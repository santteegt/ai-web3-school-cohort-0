## What I Read / Watched

- **Session keys** are limited-scope cryptographic key granted to an AI agent to act within precisely defined boundaries: contract addresses, spending limits, or time windows.
- The smart account's *validateUserOp* is the one that checks session key permissions against the policy stored in the contract state.
- **Cobo CAW Pacts** are a task-level authorization model where temporary, bounded permissions replace long-term permission grants.
  - useful when looking to automate a specific task with a defined budget, scope and time window.
  - *Core design principle*: separate steps that can be automated from steps that require human confirmation.
  - User confirms boundaries → agent executes within limits → permission auto-expires at task completion.
